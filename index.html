My Projects

During the middle of my B.Tech program, I had the opportunity to actively participate in a project that involved a site visit focused on foundation work. This experience allowed me to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application. At the site, I observed and engaged in various stages of foundation construction, including soil excavation, leveling, setting out, reinforcement placement, shuttering, and concreting. I gained hands-on exposure to how drawings and designs are implemented in the field and understood the importance of accuracy in measurements, quality control of materials, and adherence to safety standards.
